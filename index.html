You Owe Me

"PDG" is the pinyin acronym of both Chinese "佩德館" (peideguang) and "配得感" (peidegan). The former means "a restuarant adorned with virtues"; the latter means "entitlement mentality". We hope to provide our customers a sense of deservingness or being owed a favor when little or nothing has been done to deserve special treatment.

It's the "You Owe Me" attitude!
